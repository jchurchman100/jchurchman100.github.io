athletes.gg | Bracket View

Redesigned the Smash.gg tournament bracket viewer to help players find matches more easily when at events.

Timeframe: 1 Week | Tools: Figma

Overview

The Problem

The current method players use to keep track of tournament matches is through the Smash.gg mobile website. This has proven to be too slow and suffers from poor navigation issues.

The Solution

A clearer and more efficient navigation layout along with features to help visually destinguish different events and find their target.

My Role

This project was an individual assignment. I put together the end to end deliverables while collaborating with my client on research.

Research

What are tournament attendees currently using?

To get a better look at the situation, I explored the Smash.gg mobile website, to exprience the tournament viewer myself. I was immediately able to notice some problems, such as:

  • Confusing Tabs - There was an overview page for the tournament event, but the overview tab in the bracket viewer would keep you on the brackets page.
  • Poor Screen Utilization - The login or register dialogue at the bottom of the screen take up a lot of space and isn’t necessary.
  • Slow Loading and Navigation - Pages would take an extremely long time to load, even when connected to wifi. Combined with the need to navigate through several layers to find what you’re looking made this an exhausting process.

What do the players think?

I was able to turn to twitter to find some information about how people felt about the bracket system used by the Smash.gg website. Some of the complaints were that the site is slow, a struggle to navigate, lacks notifications for your events, and it's generally hard to find what you need.

Test

Testing Scenario

While there were no usability tests done for this project, I did put together a test scenario and task list to build the prototype around.

Scenario
You are attending The Big House 9 as both a competitor and viewer and are trying to keep up with the Top 12 bracket. You missed a couple matches and want to get caught up.

Task List
  • Find Nairo’s most recent completed match.
  • View the set count between Nairo and Samsora.
  • Find the previous match Nairo completed.
  • View the match that's currently streaming.
  • Highlight all upcoming matches.
  • View the last match you played.
